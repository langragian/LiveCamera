Movies of faraway lands. Giving glimpses of a foreign culture, in a foreign tongue, yet shedding light on something near home.

Welcome to Lights, Camera, UV!

A space for those who truly live and breathe cinema — not just as entertainment, but as art, culture, and storytelling.

This group is meant for movie connoisseurs and enthusiasts who appreciate brilliant films from across the globe — be it world cinema, Hollywood, indie gems, or rare festival classics. We would also welcome people into our group who want to start on a journey of exploring them.

Here, we screen and discuss masterclass films that provoke thought, stir emotions, and offer a glimpse into faraway lands, unfamiliar cultures, and untold stories.

Weekly screenings will be held in UV’s mini theatre, featuring classics, critically acclaimed films, hidden gems, and everything in between…
